Education

MA Political Science

University of Oslo | Aug 2019 – Jun 2021

In my thesis I used matching and logistic regression to measure the effect of legal capability on the decision-making of the European Court of Justice. My thesis was awarded a “Fritt Ord” scholarship and nominated for the “Våg-å-vite” prize. Supervisor: Silje Synnøve Lyder Hermansen

BA Political Science

University of Oslo | Aug 2015 – Jun 2019

My thesis explored EU as a political cleavage with a particular focus on the differing positions of left- and right-wing political parties.
